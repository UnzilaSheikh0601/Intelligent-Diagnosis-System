About Us

MediCare Center is a smart health prediction platform that utilizes machine learning to analyze symptoms and provide probable disease outcomes. This helps individuals take timely action by knowing their condition and following the suggested precautions and medications.

Our mission is to empower people with AI-based preliminary health insights to improve awareness and bridge the gap between symptoms and consultation.
